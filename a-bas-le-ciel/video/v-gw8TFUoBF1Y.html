29 April 2017 [link youtube]

This is "a more modest manifesto" for Veganism WITHIN YOUTUBE, and the important role that youtube plays in many of our lives. Don't quit, don't be ashamed: if youtube is the only thing remotely resembling activism you've got going on in your life, recognize it for what it is (the good and the bad), don't lose your sense of purpose, and rep your set.

Chinese Politics Now: the Mass Line & Document Number Nine [黑金政治學01]

14 September 2014 [link youtube]

The most important political change that nobody's talking about: the principles of Document Number Nine (中辦發9號 / 中办发9号) and the New Mass Line Movement (群眾路線 / 群众路线) adopted by China's president Xí Jìnpíng (習近平 / 习近平).



For those who want to find more information, note that Document No. 9 is also referred to as, "關於當前意識形態領域情況的通報" / "关于当前意识形态领域情况的通报" ("Briefing on the current ideological situation", or "The situation in the ideological sphere").



Eisel Mazard, 2014.

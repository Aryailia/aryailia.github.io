Pump & Dump: James Aspey's Commodified Relationships.

24 February 2021 [link youtube]

It's shallow and it's deep AT THE SAME TIME, baby. This is a philosophical response to the questions raised by James Aspey's "Best Life" model of activism --something that has lately been (inextricably) linked to "Pump and Dump" in more than one sense of the term, as it involves both short-term investment strategies and the commodification of the activist's own intimate relationships. Each and every one of the women who came and went before (including Carly Taylor) were declared to be his one and only partner, "forever", but turned out to be pretty much "pump and dump" --and yes, that pattern indeed play out again in Panama.

An Open Letter to Professional YouTubers: CrashCourse & Vlogbrothers.

16 May 2019 [link youtube]

Have you noticed that in the public eye, historical reporting (sincerity, honesty and integrity in research) only "matters" and "has consequences" when it's World War Two for some reason? On any other topic, from any other era (especially if it's on another continent), the most poorly-informed, sub-wikipedia-level "research" is celebrated as the PBS of Youtube? Yeah, I said it: CrashCourse World History has become "the PBS of Youtube" —and, in a sense, it should be taken more seriously, and criticized more thoroughly, for that reason. The people making these videos speak out of blithe ignorance, and lead an audience of millions astray on meaningful and important issues in political history —they just don't happen to be THE ONE ISSUE (World War Two) that would get them into trouble, I guess. :-/

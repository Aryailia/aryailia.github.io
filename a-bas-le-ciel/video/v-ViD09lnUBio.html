An end to PETA's "Sexiest Vegan" competition… but…

28 July 2018 [link youtube]

My earlier video on the topic had the self-explanatory title, "PETA's "Sexy Vegan" Thing IS NOT SEXY anymore." https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Dqiws7edFGk

If you want to support the channel for $1 per month, do so on Patreon: https://www.patreon.com/a_bas_le_ciel/

You may be wondering about the image in the thumbnail. Yes, of course, the two people on the right-hand side are (1) Benedict Cumberbatch and (2) Ava DuVernay… but near the center of your field of vision, who is that? She was a vegan activist who briefly rose to fame under the name "Baby Vegan", later changed her name to "Dahliaeyes", then split up with her boyfriend, and erased most of the traces of her former digital fame. In contrast to the artificial process of PETA nominating one "sexiest vegan" after another, she's an example of someone who rose from obscurity to fame (on her own blog, basically) due to sex appeal —and yes, beauty and sex-appeal may be topics we're uncomfortable addressing within politics, but, if so, PETA's coping method is only making matters worse.

"I was wrong", says ex-Durianrider supporter, Ultrafitrunner.

15 December 2017 [link youtube]

An edited excerpt from Ultrafitrunner (who describes himself as "not vegan", and yet a supporter of the raw food diet… I shudder to think what that entails…). His channel is here: https://www.youtube.com/user/ultra100able/videos



I do find it implicitly hilarious that so many of my critics claim (as he does at the very end of this excerpt, as I've edited it) that somehow political and legal causes are trivial things compared to "focusing on one's own health" —and although I DON'T put much of a priority on my own health, I'm nevertheless in a state of much better health/strength than they are. A lot of this sounds like an echo of the old John Lennon attitude of trying to win arguments by saying, "just focus on yourself man". Well, I'm not a John-Lennon-ist: my own health and happiness are a relatively low priority… and I think that is true for most people who are passionate about politics in general (perhaps ecological causes in particular).
